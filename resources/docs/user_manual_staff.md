# Staff User Manual

Welcome to the HR Admin System. As a **Staff** member, you have a self-service portal to manage your profile, view family info, book meeting rooms, log travel, check training attendance, and track requests.

---

## 1. Dashboard Overview
Upon logging in, you will see your Dashboard. The dashboard provides a high-level summary of your upcoming room bookings, pending profile/family update requests, and any recent system notifications.

---

## 2. My Profile & My Family
You can view and manage your personal records to ensure they are up to date.

### 2.1 My Profile
1. Navigate to **My Profile** under the **Staffing** menu.
2. Review your personal details (e.g., Staff Number, Name, Email, Department, Position, and Phone Number).
3. If any details are incorrect, edit them and submit an **Update Request**. 
   > [!NOTE]
   > Profile edits do not take effect immediately; they are sent to HR admins for review and approval.

### 2.2 My Family
1. Navigate to **My Family** under the **Staffing** menu.
2. View your registered family members.
3. You can add new family members or modify existing ones. Similar to profile changes, family updates will be sent to HR for approval.

---

## 3. Meeting Rooms Booking
You can book available meeting rooms for discussions or presentations.

### 3.1 Booking a Room
1. Go to **Meeting Rooms**.
2. Select a room, date, and time slot.
3. Provide the booking details (Purpose, Number of Attendees, and any special requirements).
4. Submit the booking. The room PIC (Person In Charge) or IT Admin will review your request.

### 3.2 Managing Your Bookings
- View the status of your bookings directly on the calendar or booking list.
- You can cancel a booking if it is no longer required.
- If you need to edit an approved booking, it will require re-approval from the PIC.

---

## 4. My Travel Logs
Keep track of your official travel requests.

1. Navigate to **My Travel**.
2. Click **Create Travel Request**.
3. Enter destination, travel dates, purpose, mode of transport, and estimated costs.
4. Submit the request. HR Admins will review your travel log.

---

## 5. My Requests
Track the status of all profile and family update requests you have submitted.

1. Open **My Requests**.
2. Check the list of requests categorized by **Pending**, **Approved**, and **Rejected**.
3. If a request is rejected, you can review the comments/reasons provided by HR.

---

## 6. Training & Attendance
Monitor your professional development and training history.

### 6.1 Training Records
- Access **Training Records** to view your history of internal and external courses.
- See details like course date, duration, trainer, and whether attendance was confirmed.

### 6.2 Scanning QR Code for Attendance
- During training sessions, scan the generated session QR code with your mobile device or laptop camera to instantly confirm your attendance.

---

## 7. Account Security (MFA Setup)
Protect your account by setting up Multi-Factor Authentication.

1. Go to **System Security** -> **Account Security**.
2. Click **Setup TOTP (2FA)**.
3. Use an authenticator app (like Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator) to scan the QR code.
4. Enter the 6-digit verification code to pair your device.
5. Once configured, you will be prompted for a 2FA code during login.
