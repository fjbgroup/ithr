# Admin (HR) User Manual

As an **Admin (HR)** user, you are responsible for managing employee records, tracking update requests, managing training programs, overseeing meeting room bookings, reviewing travel logs, and maintaining master data.

---

## 1. Staffing Management
You have full access to view, update, and manage employee records.

### 1.1 Staff List
- Go to **Staffing** -> **Staff List**.
- **Add Staff**: Click **Add Staff** to create a single employee profile. Fill in the staff number, name, department, position, company, status, phone number, etc.
- **Bulk Action**: Select multiple staff members to perform bulk deletions or status updates.
- **Import/Export**: Download the excel template, fill in staff details, upload the file to preview, and import it into the system. You can also export the current list to Excel.
- **Archive/Deactivate**: Toggle the status of a staff member to make them active or inactive. Inactive staff cannot make bookings or record attendance.

### 1.2 Family Info
- Go to **Staffing** -> **Family Info**.
- Manage family relations for all staff.
- Search by staff name or family name.
- Supports bulk delete, and template-based Excel import/export.

### 1.3 Registry Report
- Go to **Staffing** -> **Registry Report**.
- Filter records by Company, Department, and Active Status.
- Export the filtered report to Excel for official reporting.

### 1.4 Industrial Relations (IR) Records
- Go to **Staffing** -> **IR Records**.
- Document and manage sensitive employee cases, warning letters, or industrial relation logs.
- > [!IMPORTANT]
  > IR records are confidential and restricted to Admin (IT), Admin (HR), and the CEO only.

### 1.5 Archived Staff
- Go to **Staffing** -> **Archived Staff**.
- Retrieve or view records of staff members who have left the company or are inactive.

---

## 2. Update Requests Approval
When staff members submit changes to their profiles or family records, they appear as pending requests.

1. Go to **Update Requests** (you will see a count of pending requests in the sidebar).
2. Click **View Details** to compare the current value vs. the proposed change.
3. Click **Approve** to apply the change directly to the user profile, or **Reject** with a comment.
4. Use **Dismiss** to discard outdated requests.

---

## 3. Training & QR Attendance
Manage training programs and monitor employee training stats.

### 3.1 Course Management
- Go to **Training** -> **Training Records**.
- Create courses with titles, training providers, categories (Internal/External), hours, and description.
- To manage attendees, search for staff and add them to the participant list.

### 3.2 QR Attendance Scan & Projector Mode
- For a specific course, click **QR Page** or **Generate QR Code**.
- Open the **Projector Mode** on a large screen during the training. The projector view automatically refreshes as staff scan the QR code to sign in.

### 3.3 Training Reports
- Go to **Training** -> **Training Report**.
- Review training hour metrics, external vs. internal split, and category charts.
- Export training logs and summary sheets to Excel.

---

## 4. Meeting Rooms & PIC Approvals
Review meeting room requests and bookings.

- Go to **Meeting Rooms**.
- As an Admin, you can review bookings, approve held slots, or resolve overlapping conflicts.
- If you are a Person In Charge (PIC) of a specific room, you will receive notifications for pending bookings. Approve or reject requests accordingly.

---

## 5. Travel Records
- Go to **Travel Records**.
- View a consolidated list of all employee travel logs.
- Filter by destination, purpose, requester, and dates.

---

## 6. Master Data Maintenance
Maintain structural integrity of dropdown parameters in forms.

1. Go to **System Security** -> **Master Data**.
2. Manage entities like:
   - Departments
   - Companies
   - Positions
   - Locations / Cities
3. Use edit/delete actions. Note that you should not delete items that are already linked to active staff.
